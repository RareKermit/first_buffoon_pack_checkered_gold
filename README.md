# first_buffoon_pack_checkered_gold
Balatro:  Evaluating whether or not you take the first buffoon pack on Checkered Deck, Gold Stake by estimating likely pack value.

holo, foil, poly, and negative were not accounted for in this evaluation.

Results (roughly):

"good" outcome: ~75%  ("good" means clean rebate which is actually "great", but also includes eternal wrathful and perishable abstract)

"good" OR having 5 or more dollars after taking pack:  ~87.5%

Conclusion:  You take the pack!  It's good!
